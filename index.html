Good Vibes Roatan

Your Private Jungle Escape Near West End's Half Moon Bay

Nestled in 3 acres of maintained jungle, our two private 1-bedroom units offer a quiet, natural vibe just a short walk from West End's Half Moon Bay. Each unit has its own kitchen and bathroom, natural breezes, solar powered with battery system and back-up generator, and access to a covered deck with beautiful jungle views.

Perched gently in the trees on a peaceful hilltop, this is a place to relax around the bonfire and grilling area. We are truly blessed to share this sanctuary with you — moments away from Roatan’s rich experiences and beauty.

We know Roatan and most tour operators personally and can help you set up any adventure you desire — from exploring South Shore Zip Line to charter fishing guided by family and friends.

Want to volunteer? Help is always welcome at the West End Community Center. Interested in music or art? We might lend an instrument or set you up with a canvas. Hosting an intimate jam night is possible at The House of Good Vibes — experience Roatan like no other.
