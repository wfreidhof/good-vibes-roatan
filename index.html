Welcome to Your Hilltop Jungle Retreat

Perched on a peaceful hilltop and nestled within 3 acres of lush, maintained jungle, our two private 1-bedroom units offer a perfect blend of seclusion and convenience. Each unit has its own kitchen and bathroom, natural breezes, and is solar powered with a battery system and back-up generator.

Step out onto the spacious covered deck surrounded by greenery, or gather around the cozy bonfire and grilling area for relaxing evenings under the stars.

Just a short walk from West End’s beautiful Half Moon Bay, you’ll feel right at home in this serene tropical setting.

Things to Do on Roatan — All Moments Away

  • Explore the vibrant coral reefs with snorkeling and scuba diving
  • Relax on the white sandy beaches of West End and Half Moon Bay
  • Discover local culture and crafts at the artisan markets
  • Enjoy fresh seafood and tropical cuisine at nearby restaurants
  • Take a boat tour to see dolphins, turtles, and island wildlife
  • Zip through the jungle canopy at South Shore Zip Line
  • Experience lively nightlife with bars and live music venues
  • Hike through jungle trails and enjoy panoramic island views
  • Sip a cold drink at the world-famous Sundowner’s Beach Bar — where I play live music every Tuesday evening for Taco Tunesday

Experience Roatan Like a Local

We’ve lived on Roatan for over a decade, and my wife’s family is from the island. We know the community and most local tour operators personally, and we’re happy to help you arrange any experience you’re dreaming of — whether it’s a guided fly fishing trip with my brother-in-law, who knows these waters like the back of his hand, or simply pointing you toward the perfect dive shop or tucked-away local restaurant.

Want to give back while you’re here? Help is always welcomed at the West End Community Center, a small local nonprofit doing meaningful work with children and families in the area. Just ask us how you can get involved.

The House of Good Vibes

This is a space to unwind, to connect, and to experience Roatan like no other.
